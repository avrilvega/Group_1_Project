About Customer Dataset

This dataset is sourced from Kaggle.com and contains customer details related to a bank's operations.

Dataset Variables:

  • Row Number: Sequential row number in the dataset
  • CustomerId: Unique identifier for each customer
  • Surname: Family name of the customer
  • CreditScore: Credit score of the customer
  • Geography: Location (country) of the customer
  • Gender: Gender of the customer
  • Age: Age of the customer
  • Tenure: Number of years the customer has been with the bank
  • Balance: Account balance of the customer
  • NumOfProducts: Number of bank products used by the customer
  • HasCrCard: Indicates if the customer has a credit card (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
  • IsActiveMember: Indicates if the customer is an active member (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
  • EstimatedSalary: Estimated salary of the customer
  • Exited: Indicates if the customer has exited the bank (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
